Majestic! Stealthy! Magnificient!
The Bengal tiger is a majestic creature, an apex predator of the animal kingdom. It is also one of the most mysterious and elusive animals to be found in the wild. Its migratory patterns and natural habitats remain shrouded in mystery, but what we do know about this remarkable species is truly awe-inspiring.
